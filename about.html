About Us

History and Overview

We are a small Melbourne based software company with offices also in Sydney, Brisbane and Adelaide. Since our founding in 1993, we have proudly being providing top quality and best practices software solutions to varies clients in Australia.

We offer varies software solutions to our customers including the installation and maintaince of IT systems infrastructure such as networking, virtual machines and servers that are Windows and Linux based. However, we specalise in Linux systems and this is where we started at in the 1990s.

Since then we have expanded into providing web-based and mobile-based enterprise application development and web design services. We have recently started looking into providing automation services for varies business processes such as administration tasks.

Corporate Responsibility

We are passionate about supporting the community that were work in and in promoting inclusion, wellbeing and sustainablity. In the following ways we are helping out in our local communities:

  • Several times a year a group of our software engineers go out to volunteer themselves to run code camps and classes.
  • Once a year each engineer spends a day volunteering in the community.
  • For each office, we have installed solar panels to save on power usage and developed IT infrastructure around this solar technology to maximise their effectiveness.

Funding

The company started out as being privately funded by the owner and since then we have obtained further funding from varies private individuals and capital investment firms.
